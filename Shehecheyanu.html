Shehecheyanu

/ בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה Baruch atah Blessed are you /
/ יְיָ אֱלֹהֵינוּ Adonai Eloheinu Adonai our God /
/ מֶלֶךְ הָעוֹלָם Melech Ha’olam Ruler of the Universe /
/ שֶׁהֶחֱיָנוּ shehecheyanu who has given us life, /
/ וְקִיְּמָנוּ v’kiy’manu sustained us, /
/ וְהִגִּיעָנוּ v’higi’anu and has brought us /
/ לַזְּמַן הַזֶּה laz’man hazeh to this time. /
