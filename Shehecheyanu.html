שהחינו Shehecheyanu

/ בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ אֱלֹהֵינוּ מֶלֶךְ הָעוֹלָם /
/ שֶׁהֶחֱיָנוּ וְקִיְּמָנוּ וְהִגִּיעָנוּ לַזְּמַן הַזֶּה /
/ /
/ Baruch atah Adonai Eloheinu Melech Ha'olam /
/ shehecheyanu v'kiy'manu v'higi'anu laz'man hazeh /
/ /
/ Blessed are you Adonai, our God, Ruler of the Universe, /
/ who has given us life, sustained us, and brought us to this time. /
/ /
