Psalm 27:4
/ אַחַת שָׁאַלְתִּי מֵאֵת יְיָ Achat sha'al'ti me'et Adonai One thing I ask from Adonai /
/ אֹותָהּ אֲבַקֵּשׁ otah avakesh this thing I seek – /
/ /
/ שִׁבְתִּי בְּבֵית יְיָ shiv'ti b'veit Adonai to dwell in the house of Adonai /
/ כׇּל יְמֵי חַיַּי kol y'mei chayai all the days of my life /
/ /
/ לַחֲזֹות בְּנֹעַם יְיָ lachazot b'no'am Adonai to behold the sweetness of Adonai /
/ וּלְבַקֵּר בְּהֵיכָלֹו ul'vaker b'heichalo and to gaze on His temple /
